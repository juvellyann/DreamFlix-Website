MINE

The series glimpses into the high society's lifestyle and follows strong and ambitious women who are trying to find the real 'mine'. Seo Hi-soo (Lee Bo-young), a former top actress and now the second daughter-in-law of Hyowon Group, and Jung Seo-hyun (Kim Seo-hyung), the first daughter-in-law of Hyowon Group, are trying to find their true identities.

Cast: Lee Bo-Young, Kim Seo-Hyung, Ok Ja-Yeon, Jung Yi-Seo, Lee Hyun-Wook, Cha Hak-Yeon

Genre: Thriller, Mystery, Drama

Total Episode: 16

Released Date: May 8, 2021 - June 27, 2021

Maturity Rating: +16
