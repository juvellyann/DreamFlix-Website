MY NAME

My Name is a Netflix series portraying Yoon Ji Woo's revenge toward her father's murderer. Following her father's death, she works for powerful crime gang led by Choi Moo Jin. He helps to reveal the murder of Ji Woo's father. Meanwhile, Ji Woo joins police department under Moo Jin's instruction. Later, she works for drug investigation unit of police department with her partner Detective Jeon Pil Do.

Cast: Han So-Hee, Park Hee-Soon, Ahn Bo-Hyun, Lee Hak-Joo, Kim Sang-Ho, Chang Ryul, Yoon Kyung-Ho

Genre: Action, Thriller, Crime, Noir

Total Episode: 8

Released Date: October 15, 2021

Maturity Rating: +16
