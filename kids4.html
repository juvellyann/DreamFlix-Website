SING 2

There's just one hitch: They first have to persuade the world's most reclusive rock star--played by global music icon Bono, in his animated film debut--to join them.

Cast: Matthew McConaughey, Reese Witherspoon, Scarlett Johansson

Genre: Comedy, Musical, Kids & Family, Animation

Runtime: 1h 50m

Maturity Rating: 13+ (some rude material, mild peril/violence)
